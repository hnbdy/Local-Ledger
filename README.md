# Local-Ledger

Local Leger is  a privacy first personal finance tracker for recording expenses, managing bills, and reviewing spending patterns without relying on cloud-based finace apps.

The project focuses on privacy, simplicity, and customization. Financial data is stored locally by default, giving users more control over how their records are managed, backed up, and analyzed.

## Features

### Dashboard

* View an overview of personal financial records
* Check recent income, expenses, deadlines, and exchange-related information in one place
* Provide a clear summary for daily financial management

### Income & Expenses

* Record income and expense transactions
* Organize records by date, amount, category, account, and notes
* Review daily and monthly spending history

### Analytics

* Analyze spending patterns by category and time period
* View summaries of income, expenses, and balance changes
* Support future data visualization for financial trends

### Financial Deadlines

* Track recurring financial tasks and deadlines
* Record items such as credit card payments, subscriptions, bills, rent, tuition, and other scheduled financial events
* Help users avoid missing important payment dates

### Currency Exchange

* Record currency exchange activities
* Track exchange-related limits, such as annual foreign exchange quotas
* Manage personal cross-currency financial records, especially for international students or people living across countries

### Exchange Rates

* Record or update exchange rates for different currencies
* Support future exchange rate history and conversion tools
* Help compare exchange timing and currency movement

### Data Management

* Store financial data locally by default
* Import and export records for backup or further analysis
* Keep the project flexible for future extensions such as CSV import, database storage, and personal finance reports
